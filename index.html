Easy Recipes

Perfect Scrambled Eggs

A simple breakfast that anyone can master in just 5 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons milk (optional)

Instructions

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk well
  2. Add milk, salt, and pepper
  3. Heat butter in a non-stick pan over low heat
  4. Pour in eggs and stir gently as they cook
  5. Remove from heat when eggs are still slightly wet

Simple Pasta with Garlic

A classic Italian dish that requires only a few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic, sliced thin
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan
  3. Add garlic and cook until golden
  4. Add cooked pasta and toss
  5. Season with pepper flakes and parsley

Kitchen Tips for Beginners

Essential Kitchen Tools

  • Sharp chef's knife
  • Cutting board
  • Non-stick pan
  • Wooden spoon
  • Measuring cups

Basic Cooking Tips

  1. Read the entire recipe before starting
  2. Prepare all ingredients first
  3. Taste your food as you cook
  4. Keep your knives sharp
  5. Clean as you go

Food Safety

Important: Always wash your hands before cooking and keep raw meat separate from other ingredients.
